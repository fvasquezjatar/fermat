== Chapter 15: Fermat Shopping Platform: 

This platform is designed to allow the interaction between the actors of traditional consumer market of products and services. +


The new identities introduced are:
[horizontal]
Shop :: to identify shop owners who accept crypto currency in payment of their products
Brand :: for brands that accept crypto currency in their retailers network
Retail :: for brands retailers that accept crypto currency


=== New components
Some of the new components added to the framework by this platform are those that offer the following functionalities:

* Purchase Transmission (purchase of products paid with crypto currency)
* Purchase and Sale transactions 
* Wallets: Shop Wallet, Brand Wallet and Retail Wallet

=== Shop Wallet 
This wallet includes these specific new functionalities:

* To receive payments in crypto currency
* To connect a purchase bill of the shop with a crypto request to cover the fiat currency amount billed
